My Story

From the land of my forefathers to the shores of the melting pot we call America this is my life, the life of a teenage Asian immigrant in America. Born in a local hospital in his home town of Ginh Long in Vietnam on September 15, 2001 and shortly flown to the U.S 3 years later. Raised in Memphis,TN Phat attended Bruce Elementary School. After finishing his elementary education his family relocated to Bartlett,TN where he resides now. He attended Bon Lin Middle school and later on went to Bartlett Highschool. In which he attends Mrs. Sinclair's Web Design class and is typing this right now a day late because he had to take AP Exams.
